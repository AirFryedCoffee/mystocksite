Relative Markets

The Objective

This site was created so that a stock of the user's choice could be compared to the S&P 500. Input a ticker and your stock will be compared to the S&P

Some market participants believe that comparing certain stocks to the S&P 500 is a way to gauge the relative strength or weakness to compare to the overall market.

For example, if a trader were expecting to trade AAPL, one thing that he or she might take into account is how AAPL is performing compared to the market.

It is common to examine the difference in performance on a chart. It is worth taking note if either stock has a large spike and or decline, and the other stock does not act the same

If AAPL shows a steady uptrend or a large spike, and the S&P is more choppy, is slightly trending up, but maybe doesn't have the same up spike, it might be concluded that AAPL is relatively strong.
